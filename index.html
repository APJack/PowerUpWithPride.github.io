What is PUWP?

Power Up With Pride: benefiting LGBTQIA+ speedrunners, featuring LGBTQIA+ speedrunners.

Founded in 2016, Power Up With Pride is a video gaming charity marathon for charity, created by the LGBTQIA+ speedrunning community for the community at large. During our Summer and Winter Pride events, we showcase speedruns, challenge runs, and community games, with an emphasis on LGBTQIA+ and ally runners. We do this while coming together as a community to raise money for LGBTQIA+ charities, in hopes of providing a comfortable and exciting online environment for queer folks in the gaming community.
